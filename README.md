# AI RESUME SCREENING SYSTEM

 # THE PROBLEM — HOOK          
 The average recruiter spends 6 hours every week just on screening, 42 days to hire. And still, 52% of hiring managers say finding quality candidates is their number one challenge. There has to be a better way.

# THE SOLUTION — INTRODUCE THE SYSTEM          
Introducing the AI Resume Screening System — a fully automated, intelligent hiring pipeline built on n8n that reads, scores, and decides on every CV in under 30 seconds. A candidate submits their CV through the web portal or by email. Within seconds, our AI, powered by OpenAI GPT-4o-mini, extracts every detail — skills, experience, education, communication quality — and generates a precise score out of 100. No more guessing. No more bias. Just data-driven decisions, every time.

# HOW IT WORKS — THE WORKFLOW          
Here is exactly how it works. Step one — a CV arrives by email or through the web form. The system instantly validates the file, checks for duplicates, and routes it through our security gate. The AI reads the full CV and matches it against the job description. It scores five dimensions: technical skills, years of experience, education level, communication quality, and leadership potential. The scoring engine makes a decision. Scores above 70 are shortlisted. Scores between 45 and 70 go to human review. Those below 45 receive a professional rejection. Everything is logged automatically. Every candidate gets a unique ID, a full audit trail, and is saved to the database. Your hiring manager gets an instant Slack alert and WhatsApp notification.
 
# Live Demo
Let me show you the system in action. A candidate opens the portal and fills in their details,  name, email, and uploads their CV. The whole process takes less than one minute. They select the job they are applying for, and the system handles everything else automatically. Within 30 seconds, the result appears. AI score, decision, candidate ID, and a confirmation that their full screening report is on its way by email.
Simultaneously, the hiring manager receives a Slack alert with the full candidate summary. No inbox checking required. The right people get notified instantly.
